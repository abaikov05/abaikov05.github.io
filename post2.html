 Dear friends and lovers of live music!

I am happy to inform you that very soon, on May 15, 2025, my long-awaited solo rock concert will take place in the cozy hall of “Streets of Tornakalns”! It will be an evening of sincere energy, powerful guitar riffs and soulful lyrics - everything you love in real rock, but in an unusual solo format. Below is a detailed program and a description of what to expect from me this evening.

Organized in collaboration with BaikAl Github Co.! It will be an evening of sincere energy, powerful guitar riffs and soulful lyrics.

"I Feel Like I am Drowning"

🕖 Time and place

Date: May 15, 2025 (Thursday)


Start: 19:00 (guests will be arriving from 18:30)


Location: “Streets of Tornakalns”, Torņakalna iela 69, Zemgales priekšpilsēta, Rīga, LV-1004


Tickets are already on sale on the club’s website and at the box office at the entrance.


📋 Evening program

Opening of the show

– “Wind of Change”

Emotional opening track with powerful fuzz guitars and a nod to grunge classics. Mood: mood for change and freedom.


Main set


“Night without sleep”

Lyrical ballad with gentle fingerpicking on an acoustic guitar and ingratiating background synthesizers.


“Under the sounds of rain”

Rock cut with a drum machine and deep vocals in the spirit of 90s alternative rock.


“Shards of the sky”

Instrumental experiment: guitar loops, petting lodes and live looping.


“Heartbeat”

Driven hard rock with an aggressive rhythm section and clean, “sharp” solos.


Acoustic Intermission

– “Silence in the Union”

– “Dance on the Blade”

Intimate mini-set: only vocals and acoustic guitar, so you feel every note and word.
Return to the “big stage”

“Wings of Memory”

“Beyond the Possible”
“The Last Chord”

Encore

– “Live Today”
– “Lights of the Big City”


Each track is not just a song, but a short story: about hope, loss, freedom and the pursuit of a dream.


🎸 What awaits you

Exclusive arrangements — I reworked familiar themes in new, unexpected rock arrangements.


Live-looping and effects — each sound is created right on stage, without backing tracks or backgrounds.


Interactive with the audience — at several points I invite you to sing along, clap and even contribute your own musical ideas!


Guest guitarist — a short duet on one of the compositions with a guest musician from the local scene.


Video art and light effects — to create the right atmosphere in each section of the program.


🎟 How to get there

Online tickets: Later


Club box office: daily from 12:00 to 21:00


Price: from 15 € to 25 € depending on the zone (including a free welcome drink).


I invite you to spend this evening together, share a charge of rock energy and feel every chord “on your skin”. If you are ready for a powerful sound journey — don’t miss my solo concert!


👉 Subscribe to updates on social networks and invite friends. It's going to be hot!


If you need additional details or want to know more about each song, let me know - I'll be happy to continue the description!
